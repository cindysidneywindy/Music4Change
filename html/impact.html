About Us
Music4Change was founded by a group of passionate students at Troy High School after realizing their music program needed more funds to purchase sheet music, instructor salaries, repair damaged instruments, and participation fees in parades and festivals. Through busking, Music4Change hopes to solve the problem of underfunded music programs one performance at a time.
A New Way to Fund Music
Music4Change serves to support underfunded music programs by gathering musicians to perform in public. All donations received from performances will go strictly to music programs.
